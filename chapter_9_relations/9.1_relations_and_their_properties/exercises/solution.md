#### Solution
<ol type="a">
  <li>
    a=b <br/>
    (0,0), (1, 1), (2, 2), (3, 3)
  </li>
  <li>
    a+b = 4 <br/>
    (1, 3), (2, 2), (3, 1), (4, 0)
  </li>
  <li>
    a > b <br/>
    (1, 0), <br/>
    (2, 0), (2, 1), <br/>
    (3, 0), (3, 1), (3, 2), <br/> 
    (4, 0), (4, 1), (4, 2), (4, 3)
  </li>
  <li>
    a | b $\therefore$ b is multiple of a, where $a \neq 0
  </li>
</ol>
